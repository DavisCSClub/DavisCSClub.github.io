Davis Computer Science Club

The Davis Computer Science Club (DCSC) organizes and connects UC Davis students interested in the field of computer science.

We cultivate the professional development of our members and augment their education with practical skills and projects, as well as provide a fun and supportive community.

  • Community

    We provide members a community of like-minded individuals to excel with.

  • Technical Development

    Members are given opportunities to develop their technical skills, academic and professional.

  • Weekly Events

    Members can attend weekly events to connect with other people, and learn about computer science!
